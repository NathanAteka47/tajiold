About Us

Discover our story and passion for authentic Kenyan cuisine

Welcome to Taji Eats

Taji Eats is located along Nyerere road, adjacent to Taji House opposite Makini School-Mamba area. It is an inviting open-air restaurant serving authentic Kenyan cooked delicacies.

The heartwarming taste of traditional foods and relaxing music is a haven for untold and rare Western Kenya tales and culture.

We let the flavours of heritage and home tell the story of your delicious kienyeji journey.

Join Our Team

Exciting career opportunities at Taji Eats

Taji Eats is expanding and we're looking for passionate individuals to join our team. We're currently hiring for various positions in our Bungoma location.

Restaurant Manager

Requirements:

  • At least a College Diploma/Bachelor's Degree in Business Studies/Administration, Management, Marketing, Food & Beverage Services Management or equivalent.
  • Preferably with at least 3 years of Manager specialization in food and beverages, restaurant service or equivalent is required
  • Proficient in using MS Office applications
  • Customer service oriented with strong people management skills
  • Good communication skills and leadership capability
  • Has worked on marketing related projects or possess good marketing skills
  • Knowledge on social media marketing is an added advantage

To apply, send your application to hr@tajiwater.com by SUNDAY 15TH JUNE 2025.

Chef

Requirements:

  • Diploma in culinary arts, hospitality management or professional cookery
  • At least 2 years in experience in working in a busy restaurant.
  • Experience working with traditional Kenyan cuisines will be an added advantage
  • Ability to track expenses and manage inventory/stock
  • Strong passion for the art of food design
  • Ability to lead and inspire team

To apply, send your application to hr@tajiwater.com by SUNDAY 15TH JUNE 2025.

Waiter/Waitress

Requirements:

  • Vocational Diploma in Food Beverage Services Management or equivalent
  • A minimum of six (6) months of working experience in a food, beverages, food preparation and customer service industry
  • Strong knowledge about proper food handling and practice of food sanitation
  • Flexibility and comfortable with working busy shifts
  • Fast learner and team player

To apply, send your application to hr@tajiwater.com by SUNDAY 15TH JUNE 2025.
